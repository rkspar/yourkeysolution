The AR-15 Lower Receiver

Brigade Manufacturing design team has created a match grade precision lower that can be used for the ultimate build of your tactical AR Rifle. This lower is machined from a 7075-T6 Aircraft Aluminum Billet with the latest in equipment technology available. The surface of this lower receiver is available in four different colors of Cerakote finish which is the ultimate firearms coating in the market today.

This lower features an over-sized magazine well for easier magazine changes and an integrated trigger guard for glove use and added strength.

This lower comes in two calibers 5.56 NATO and 7.62 NATO.

  • CNC machined from 7075-T6 aircraft aluminum billet stock
  • Hand bead blasted for a smooth surface finish
  • Cerakote Firearm Coatings available in four colors
  • Trigger Pinholes Small (0.154 inches)

The AR-15 Upper Receiver

Brigade Manufacturing design team has created a match grade precision upper receiver that can be used for the ultimate build of your tactical AR Rifle. This upper is machined from a 7075-T6 Aircraft Aluminum Billet with the latest in equipment technology available. The surface of this upper receiver is available in four different colors of Cerakote finish which is the ultimate firearms coating in the market today. This upper is perfect for a match set with the lower receiver that we manufacture for the creation of your ultimate AR Build.

This upper comes in two calibers 5.56 NATO and 7.62 NATO.

  • CNC machined from 7075-T6 aircraft aluminum billet stock
  • Picatinny top rail
  • Shell deflector
  • Precision threaded
  • Hand bead blasted for a smooth surface finish
  • Cerakote Firearm Coatings available in four colors
